Webinars

We host our webinars on the 3rd Tuesday of each month at 2pm Eastern Time (11am Pacific). We present unique as well as re-occurring topics on data publishing best practices, tools and new developments and you have the opportunity to join us with your questions on everything EDI during our regular “Town Halls”. Many of the webinars are recorded and available on our YouTube channel.

Contact us at info@edirepository.org with your request for a webinar topic.
