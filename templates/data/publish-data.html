Publish Data

For Authors

Login to the ezEML metadata editor to describe and submit your data (overview of ezEML). An EDI Data Curator will review the submission and respond within 24 hours.

If this is your first publication, learn about preparing for submission in the resources for data authors.

For Information Managers

Login to your EDI account to upload directly to the repository.

For information on obtaining an EDI account see the resources for information managers.
